# Team Communication Guidelines

## Language Clarity
- The majority of the Combinate team speaks English as a second language (Philippines-based)
- When drafting team-facing messages, instructions, or documentation, prioritize clarity and simplicity
- Avoid idioms, slang, or culturally specific references that may not translate well
- Use short sentences and clear structure
- When writing SOPs, guides, or instructions for the team, include concrete examples where possible

## Channel Conventions
- **Slack:** Quick updates, questions, daily collaboration
- **Teamwork.com:** Detailed task descriptions, project-level discussions, formal task assignments
- **Google Meets / Slack Huddles:** Use for alignment calls when written communication is insufficient
