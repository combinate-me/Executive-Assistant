## Brand Heart

### Purpose
Our purpose at Combinate is to empower businesses to radiate excellence in their industries and aid clients in authentically portraying their unique business identity.

### Vision
Envisioning a future where our company stands as a catalyst, empowering entrepreneurs and businesses to seamlessly connect with their target customers, and fostering meaningful and lasting online relationships.

### Mission
Our mission is to deliver top-quality services and leading-edge technology to our customers. We strive to push boundaries and set new standards, consistently providing outstanding solutions that go beyond meeting the needs of our clients.

### Values

| Value | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Innovation | We push boundaries, embracing fresh ideas to set your business apart. |
| Excellence | Dedicated to delivering work that not only meets but exceeds expectations. |
| Integrity | Built on honesty and transparency, we value trust in every interaction. |
| Collaboration | Your success is our success; we collaborate closely for a supportive partnership. |
| Forward-Thinking | Anticipating and adapting to future trends and challenges. |
| Empowerment | Enabling businesses to thrive in the digital landscape. |

### Brand Voice
Our tone is **supportive**, **empowering**, and always geared towards **fuelling your ambition**. Together, let's push boundaries, achieve milestones, and set new standards of success.

Key attributes: Encourage, Empowerment, Support, Customer Centric.

## Colour Palette

### Primary Colours

| Name | Hex | RGB | CMYK | Pantone |
|------|-----|-----|------|---------|
| Blue | `#1E43FF` | 30, 57, 255 | 89, 69, 0, 0 | 2728 C |
| Black | `#181818` | 24, 24, 24 | 0, 0, 0, 91 | Neutral Black C |

Primary colours are used to provide accessibility, simplicity, and consistency throughout all brand communications.

### Secondary Colours

Secondary colours are derived from the primary colour tint. Used for body text, backgrounds, and borders.

| Name | Hex | RGB | CMYK | Pantone |
|------|-----|-----|------|---------|
| Grey | `#4E4E4E` | 78, 78, 78 | 0, 0, 0, 84 | 425 C |
| Light Grey | `#D1D1D1` | 209, 209, 209 | 0, 0, 0, 18 | Cool Gray 1 C |
| Ghost White | `#EFF2F9` | 36, 36, 36 | 0, 0, 0, 93 | 419 C |
| Smoke White | `#FDFDFD` | 253, 253, 253 | 0, 0, 0, 4 | — |
| White | `#ffffff` | 255, 255, 255 | 0, 0, 0, 0 | — |

### Colour Proportions (60-30-10 Rule)

Follow this ratio in all brand communications to maintain consistency and accessibility.

| Proportion | Colour |
|------------|--------|
| 60% | Primary colour (Black `#181818` or White `#ffffff`) |
| 30% | Secondary colour (White or Black depending on theme) |
| 10% | Accent colour (Blue `#1E43FF`) |

**Two colour proportion contexts:**
- **Document cover, website hero, event banner** — Black dominant with Blue accent
- **Document and website inner pages** — Black dominant, White secondary, Blue accent

### Approved Background Colourways

| Background | Logo Treatment |
|------------|---------------|
| Branding Blue (`#1E43FF`) | White logo |
| Light / Ghost White | Monotone black logo |
| Dark / Black | Monotone white logo |

The reversed (white) logo can be used over images. The underlying image must be simple and not overly detailed.

### Brand Pattern

The Combinate pattern is a primary blue liquid blurred blob shape (`#1E43FF`) placed on a black background (`#181818`).

- The blob shape is positioned on the right side and covers 30% of the design
- The background is 70% black, 30% blue blob
- A heavy blur/bleed effect is applied to the blob to create the liquid look
- Used on: document covers, website hero sections, event banners
- The pattern can also be applied as an oval shape for event banners

### Applying Colours to Documents

- **White cover** — for internal documents (e.g., employment agreements)
- **Black cover** — for external documents (e.g., website proposals, user manuals, brand guidelines)
- Black can be applied to documents used by both internal and external audiences

## Typography

**Typeface: Helvetica Neue LT Pro** (licensed)

Combinate holds a limited licence for use across websites, desktops, electronic documents, banner ads, and emails.

| Weight | Use |
|--------|-----|
| Regular | Body / paragraph text |
| Italic | Emphasis within body |
| Medium | Website only |
| Bold | Headings |

**For Google applications (Google Docs, Sheets, Slides) and PandaDoc:** Use **Helvetica Neue** (the standard unlicensed version available in Google's font library).

Typestyles consist of headline styles and paragraph styles. The Medium style is only used on the website.

**Text placement principles:**
- Be expressive — big and bold, but always clear and legible
- Typography will usually be paired with a photograph or illustration
- When combining type and image, determine which is the focal point and make one much more prominent than the other
- Type and image should never take on the same prominence

## Logo

### Overview
The icosahedron represents repeated agile iterations, moving in different directions to adapt to strategic directions. The logo is the brand's most important and sacred asset — treat it with utmost respect.

### Variants

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `CMB-Logo` | Symbol (icosahedron mark) only |
| `CMB-Wordmark` | "combinate" text only |
| `CMB-Stacked` | Symbol above wordmark |
| `CMB-Horizontal` | Symbol left of wordmark |
| `CMB-Logo-spaced` | Symbol with extra clear space (PNG only) |
| `*-NEG` variants | White/reversed versions for dark backgrounds |

**Formats:** SVG (preferred for digital/web), PNG (transparent background, general digital), JPG (white background), EPS (print/press)

All files live in `branding/Logo/` organised by format (EPS, JPG, PNG, SVG).

### Colourways

| Colourway | Usage |
|-----------|-------|
| Branding Colour (Blue `#1E43FF`) | Primary brand application |
| Monotone on Light | Black logo on light backgrounds |
| Monotone on Dark | White logo on dark backgrounds |

### Clear Space

The logo must always be surrounded by minimum clear space separating it from other objects and elements.

**Horizontal logo** (x indicator = height of the "e" in wordmark):
- All sides: 1.5x

**Symbol only** (x indicator = symbol height):
- All sides: 0.5x

This clear space must be kept free of all types, graphic elements, rules, and detailed areas within illustrations and photography.

### Minimum Size

| Context | Minimum |
|---------|---------|
| Print | 40mm width |
| Digital | 150px width |

Only use minimum size when space is genuinely limited. Never set smaller than these sizes.

### Logo Placement

| Position | Use |
|----------|-----|
| Top left | Default — for websites, documents, email signatures, business cards |
| Centre | Email marketing headers only |
| Bottom left | Footer sections (website, stationery) |

### Incorrect Use — Never Do These

- Do not alter the logo in any way
- Do not apply drop shadow or effects
- Do not rotate the logo
- Do not stretch the logo
- Do not use on non-approved colour backgrounds (any colour outside the approved colourways)
- Do not use symbol and wordmark separately without cause

## Document Templates

All client-facing Google Docs must use the **Combinate branded template**. Never use plain `createDocument`.

| Format | Template / Notes |
|--------|-----------------|
| Google Docs (external) | Template ID: `12TovrIc6MuTjl0dvRycqR56HWssYISNvdnrI_4CwW8U` — black cover |
| Google Docs (internal) | Internal template available in Google Docs gallery — white cover |
| Google Sheets | Sheet template available in Google Sheets gallery |
| Presentations (16:9) | Internal and external templates in Google Slides gallery |
| Proposals / SOWs | PandaDoc template library |

### Google Doc Creation Workflow (always follow this order)

1. Use `createDocumentFromTemplate` with template ID `12TovrIc6MuTjl0dvRycqR56HWssYISNvdnrI_4CwW8U`
   - Set `newTitle` to the document name
   - Set `parentFolderId` to the correct Drive folder
   - Replace cover placeholders: `"Document Title"` and `"Document Subtitle"`
2. The template creates a branded cover (dark blue header, Combinate logo, title/subtitle)
3. The document body contains sample content — delete it
4. Find the document end index via the error from `deleteRange` with a large endIndex (e.g. 9999) — the error message contains the actual max
5. Delete from index 109 to (max endIndex - 1) to clear all sample content while keeping the cover table
6. Insert a newline at index 108 using `insertText` to create a trailing paragraph after the table
7. Insert full body content at index 108 using `insertText`
8. Apply heading styles using `applyParagraphStyle` with `textToFind` for each heading (HEADING_1, HEADING_2, etc.)

**Important:** `appendMarkdown` fails when the document ends with a table. Always use `insertText` at index 108 after the cover table. The cover table occupies indices 4–108 in all documents from this template.

## Email Standards

All emails drafted for Shane must be:

- **Format:** HTML (`text/html`) — never plain text
- **Styling:** Bold for emphasis, bullet points for lists, clear paragraph breaks
- **Signature:** Always append Shane's HTML signature — read `branding/email-signature.html` and append it to the end of every email body. Gmail does NOT automatically apply signatures to API-created drafts.
- **Threading:** When creating a draft reply, thread it into the existing email thread where one exists.

### Email Signature

Shane's signature is at `branding/email-signature.html`. Always read the file — never reconstruct the signature from memory.

Format: Logo mark (left-aligned) | Name | Title | Phone | Address | Website

## Imagery Guidelines

### Photography
Crafted to reflect realness and human-centric values. Authentic visual storytelling to connect with the audience.

- Candid, relatable, real-world contexts
- High quality, well-lit, clear focus
- Avoid overly staged or corporate-looking shots

### Illustrations
- Clean, flat vector style
- Blue hue referencing brand colours
- Used to showcase the services Combinate can deliver

### Showcase / Mockups
- Real device mockups (smartphones, laptops, wearables) — not generic placeholders
- Used in proposals, sales materials, and case studies

### Event Banners
- Black backdrop with the brand pattern (blue blob, 30% coverage)
- 20% text, maximum 30% blue pattern
- Can use real images or AI-generated objects
- Blue blurred effect can be positioned top right or bottom right

### Google Meet Backgrounds
Four standard backgrounds are available for team use:
1. Dark Theme
2. Primary Blue Solid
3. Light Theme
4. Primary Blue Pattern

All include the Combinate logo and `www.combinate.me` branding.

## Writing Tone (External-Facing Content)

- Professional, confident, and knowledgeable — sound like an expert who knows their craft
- Human and approachable — never robotic or overly formal
- Supportive and empowering — geared towards fuelling the client's ambition
- Direct — no hedging, no filler phrases
- No emojis
- No em dashes
- No opening filler like "I hope this email finds you well" or "Just circling back"
