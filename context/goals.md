## Priorities as Goals

No formal quarterly goals are set. The following priorities serve as the working goals for this period:

1. **Grow the sales pipeline** - Consistently convert bark.com leads into proposals and close new medium-to-large projects
2. **Deliver projects on time and on budget** - Reduce bugs, improve delivery quality, and maintain client satisfaction on active large projects
3. **Drive AI adoption** - Get the team actively using AI tools in their daily work, establish measurement and tracking for AI usage and output improvements
4. **Establish team standards** - Put proper processes and tools in place across design, development, and operations
5. **Resolve client friction** - Address cost concerns on active projects and maintain strong client relationships
